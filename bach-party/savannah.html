Bachelorette Party Ideas in Savannah, GA

Plan the perfect weekend with charming streets, memorable group experiences, and fun things to do across Savannah.

Things To Do for a Savannah Bachelorette

Savannah is a favorite destination for bachelorette parties thanks to its walkable historic districts, beautiful scenery, lively nightlife, and relaxed Southern charm. Whether your group wants daytime fun, unique local experiences, or a memorable night out, Savannah offers plenty of ways to celebrate together.

1. River Street Stroll

Walk along River Street, enjoy the waterfront views, browse local shops, and stop for drinks or snacks with your group.

2. Rooftop Drinks

Take in city views while enjoying cocktails at one of Savannah’s rooftop bars, perfect for group photos and a fun evening start.

3. Tybee Island Day Trip

Head to Tybee Island for a beach day filled with sunshine, ocean views, and a relaxing coastal break from downtown.

4. Brunch in the Historic District

Start the day with mimosas, coffee, and brunch in one of Savannah’s charming restaurants or cafés.

5. Private Group Class

Book a private class such as yoga, painting, or another group-friendly activity to make the trip feel more personal.

6. Savannah Boat Cruise

Enjoy time on the water with your group and take in a different view of the city during a scenic cruise.

7. Spa Day

Slow things down with a spa day featuring massages, facials, and a relaxing break from the busy weekend schedule.

8. Group Dinner Experience

Reserve a memorable dinner for the whole group and enjoy Savannah’s well-known food scene in a celebratory setting.

9. Private In-Home Entertainment

For groups wanting something different, private entertainment at your rental can add a fun and personalized touch to the celebration.

10. Night Out Downtown

Wrap up the evening with music, dancing, and Savannah nightlife in the downtown area.

Plan Your Savannah Bachelorette Weekend

From waterfront walks to group activities and nightlife, Savannah has everything you need for a memorable celebration.
