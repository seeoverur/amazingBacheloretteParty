Bachelorette Party Ideas in Charleston, SC

Plan the perfect weekend with unforgettable experiences, great food, and fun group activities in the Lowcountry.

Things To Do for a Charleston Bachelorette

Charleston is one of the top destinations for bachelorette parties thanks to its mix of coastal charm, vibrant nightlife, and laid-back daytime experiences. Whether your group is looking for relaxing brunches, exciting group activities, or unique private experiences, there’s something here for every style of celebration.

1. King Street Shopping & Cafés

Spend the afternoon exploring boutiques, grabbing coffee, and enjoying the lively atmosphere of Charleston’s most iconic street.

2. Rooftop Bar Hopping

Take in skyline views while enjoying cocktails at some of Charleston’s best rooftop bars, perfect for sunset photos.

3. Beach Day at Folly or Isle of Palms

Relax by the ocean, soak up the sun, and enjoy a refreshing escape just minutes from downtown.

4. Brunch Crawl

Start your day with mimosas, coffee, and delicious brunch dishes at some of the city’s favorite spots.

5. Private Group Yoga

Book a private yoga session for your group to relax, recharge, and start the day feeling refreshed.

6. Harbor Cruise

Enjoy scenic views of the Charleston harbor while spending quality time with your group on the water.

7. Spa Day

Pamper yourselves with massages, facials, and relaxing treatments for a luxurious group experience.

8. Group Dinner Experience

Reserve a table at one of Charleston’s top restaurants for a memorable night of food and celebration.

9. Private In-Home Entertainment

Bring the fun directly to your rental with a private group experience. Options like Michael At Your Place offer a unique way to add energy and laughter to your celebration in a comfortable setting.

10. Night Out Downtown

End the day with music, dancing, and a vibrant nightlife scene throughout downtown Charleston.

Plan Your Charleston Bachelorette Weekend

From relaxing beach days to exciting group activities, Charleston offers everything you need for an unforgettable celebration.
