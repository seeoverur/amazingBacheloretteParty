Things To Do for a Las Vegas Bachelorette

Las Vegas is one of the most popular bachelorette destinations in the country, known for its high-energy nightlife, luxury pool parties, entertainment, and endless things to do. Whether your group wants a full party weekend or a mix of relaxation and excitement, Vegas offers something for every type of celebration.

1. Pool Party Experience

Spend the day at a Vegas pool party with DJs, drinks, and a high-energy atmosphere perfect for celebrating with your group.

2. Las Vegas Strip Walk

Walk the Strip, explore iconic hotels, take photos, and enjoy the nonstop energy that makes Vegas unique.

3. Rooftop & Lounge Drinks

Start the evening with cocktails at a rooftop or upscale lounge with incredible views of the city lights.

4. Brunch on the Strip

Recover and recharge with a group brunch featuring mimosas, coffee, and a lively Vegas vibe.

5. Live Show Experience

Catch a high-energy live performance or stage show for a fun group outing that adds excitement to the weekend.

6. Spa Day

Balance the weekend with a relaxing spa visit including massages, facials, and downtime before the night begins.

7. Group Dinner Experience

Reserve a memorable dinner at one of Vegas’s well-known restaurants and enjoy a celebratory meal together.

8. Private In-Home Entertainment

For a more personal experience, private entertainment at your suite or rental can add a unique and fun touch.

9. Nightclub Experience

Dance the night away at one of Vegas’s famous nightclubs and enjoy the city’s unmatched nightlife scene.

10. Day Trip or Excursion

Take a break from the Strip with a nearby excursion or scenic outing for a different kind of group experience.
