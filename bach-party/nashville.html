Bachelorette Party Ideas in Nashville, TN

Plan an unforgettable weekend with live music, group activities, great food, and fun things to do across Nashville.

Things To Do for a Nashville Bachelorette

Nashville is one of the most popular destinations for bachelorette parties thanks to its live music scene, energetic nightlife, Southern food, and group-friendly atmosphere. Whether your group wants daytime activities, rooftop drinks, or a memorable night out, Nashville has plenty of ways to celebrate in style.

1. Broadway Bar Hopping

Walk along Broadway, enjoy live music, and experience the energy of Nashville’s most iconic nightlife district.

2. Rooftop Drinks

Head to a rooftop bar for skyline views, cocktails, and a fun setting for group photos before the night begins.

3. Brunch with the Group

Start the day with brunch, coffee, and a lively atmosphere at one of Nashville’s many popular spots.

4. Pedal Tavern or Party Ride

See the city in a fun, social way with a group ride that keeps the party moving through downtown.

5. Private Group Class

Book a private activity like yoga, dance, or a creative workshop to make the weekend feel even more personalized.

6. Live Music Experience

Enjoy the city’s signature music scene with local performances, songwriter rounds, and energetic venues.

7. Spa Day

Take time to relax with a spa visit, massages, or a wellness break before another night out.

8. Group Dinner Reservation

Plan a celebratory dinner and enjoy Nashville’s food scene with the whole group gathered together.

9. Private In-Home Entertainment

For groups wanting something different, entertainment brought to your rental can add a memorable and customized touch to the celebration.

10. Night Out Downtown

Finish the evening with dancing, music, and the excitement of Nashville nightlife.

Plan Your Nashville Bachelorette Weekend

From live music to brunch, nightlife, and group activities, Nashville offers everything you need for a fun celebration.
